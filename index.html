Company Overview

Niwa Shoes Co., Ltd. is an omni-channel women’s fashion footwear retailer and wholesaler headquartered in Bangkok. The company operates 30 retail outlets in premium shopping malls across Greater Bangkok, supported by a fully integrated e-commerce ecosystem (Shopee, Lazada, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and its own webstore), as well as a growing export network serving ASEAN markets.
